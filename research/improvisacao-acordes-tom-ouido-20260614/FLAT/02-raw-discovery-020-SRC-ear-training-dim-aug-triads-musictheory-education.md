Sing the triad in root position and decide whether the triad is major, minor, diminished or augmented."

## Primary claims
- Four-quality triad discrimination builds on prior inversion-recognition skills (Ch. 6.10).
- Exercises progress: root-position only → harmonic-only playback → type+position combined.
- Singing/re-solfegeing heard triads into root position is a prescribed technique for inner hearing.
- Notation shorthand (ma, mi, d, a, 6, 6/4) integrates ear training with theory literacy.
- Augmented triad inversions need not be specified due to symmetrical structure.
