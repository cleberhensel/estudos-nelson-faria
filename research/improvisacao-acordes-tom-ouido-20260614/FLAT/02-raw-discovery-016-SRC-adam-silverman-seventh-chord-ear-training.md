## Verbatim snippets / quotes

> "To identify seventh chord qualities by ear: find a partner and take turns quizzing each other. On a piano, arpeggiate chords from bottom to top, then play as a block."

> "Different kinds of seventh chords are based on triad 'cores':"
> - Major triad → dominant 7 (minor 7th outer) vs major 7 (major 7th outer)
> - Minor triad → minor 7 vs minor/major 7
> - Diminished triad → half-diminished (minor 7 outer) vs fully-diminished (diminished 7 outer)

## Primary claims

- Identificar acorde de 7ª = triade núcleo + intervalo da 7ª.
- Associações estilísticas ajudam: dom7 "bluesy", maj7 "jazzy", mMaj7 "picante".
- Arpejar antes de bloquear acelera discriminação auditiva.
