## Citações relevantes

> "Shell voicings are better known as 3-note chords (or guide tone chords). They are made up of the most essential notes (root, third and seventh) that define a chord (the fifth is omitted)."

> "They can very useful for beginner guitarists who want to explore basic guitar comping rhythms."

> "3-note shell voicings are widely used to mix chords and walking bass lines on guitar. They are very simple to play and require only three fingers. They are also very useful to connect fast walking bass lines while underlying the harmony of a tune."

> Tipos essenciais: Major 7 (1-3-7), Minor 7 (1-b3-b7), Dominant 7 (1-3-b7), Diminished 7 / Minor 6.

---

## Claims extraídos

1. Shell voicings = **guide tone chords** — nomenclatura alternativa comum.
2. Apenas **3 tipos de 7ª** + dim/min6 cobrem repertório jazz funcional inicial.
3. Posições por root na 6ª, 5ª e 4ª cordas — sistema de 3 shapes por qualidade.
4. **Integração baixo+acorde**: shells permitem alternar nota de baixo solta com acorde (técnica Freddie Green / fingerstyle jazz).
5. Dim7 e min6 compartilham mesmas posições (bb7 = 6).
