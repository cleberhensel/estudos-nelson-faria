# [SRC-050]: Identifying Standard Pop Chord Progressions — Ethan Hein

**URL**: https://www.ethanhein.com/wp/2024/identifying-standard-pop-chord-progressions/
**Captured**: 2026-06-14
**Type**: blog / pedagogy (NYU aural skills)

## Verbatim snippets / quotes

> "A huge percentage of current mainstream pop and rock songs are built on the four-legged stool of the I, IV, V and vi chords."

> "There's a particularly inescapable sequence that my NYU colleagues call the singer-songwriter progression: I, V, vi, IV, which in C is C, G, Am, F."

> "The Doo-Wop progression is the same chords as the Axis progression, just in a different order: I-vi-IV-V."

> "The hopscotch progression is yet another re-ordering of the Axis and doo-wop chords: IV-V-vi-I."

> "We are practicing identifying pop schemas, that is, chord sequences and loops that occur commonly in various kinds of Anglo-American top 40, rock, R&B and related styles."

## Primary claims

- Pop mainstream atual repousa quase inteiramente em I, IV, V, vi — quatro acordes diatônicos.
- Três schemas principais: singer-songwriter (I–V–vi–IV), doo-wop (I–vi–IV–V), hopscotch (IV–V–vi–I).
- Identificação por schema é método de aural skills universitário (NYU).
- Mesmos acordes, ordens diferentes = assinaturas distintas reconhecíveis pelo ouvido.
- Treino focado em loops/schema, não em acordes individuais.

## Relevance to charter Q4

Metodologia de internalização explícita: praticar identificação de schemas em aural skills. Complementa dados Hooktheory com abordagem pedagógica. Diretamente aplicável a músicos brasileiros que acompanham pop internacional e precisam reconhecer progressões em tempo real.
