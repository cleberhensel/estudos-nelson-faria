## Verbatim snippets / quotes

> "Functional harmony in tonal music centers around the idea that tonic serves as the 'home base' in a key and chords built on each scale degree in the scale have predictable relationships with each other and predictable movements as they ultimately progress towards tonic."

> "The predominant category comes before the dominant category on its way to tonic. The two chords in the predominant category are IV and ii."

> "Moving from predominant to dominant to tonic is one the most fundamental and frequently used progressions in music."

## Primary claims

- Progressões "esperadas" vs. "obscurecidas" afetam drasticamente a percepção de tonalidade.
- Categoria predominante (IV, ii) precede dominante (V, vii°) na resolução à tônica.
- Análise funcional deve influenciar prática: categorizar acordes desconhecidos acelera aprendizado.

## Relevance to charter Q1

Modelo acadêmico de categorias (predominante/dominante/tônica) para predição em tempo real.
