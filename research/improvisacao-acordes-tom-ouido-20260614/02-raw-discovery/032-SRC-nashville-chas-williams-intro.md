## Verbatim snippets / quotes

> "One of the main benefits of a number chart is that it can be played in any key without transposing or rewriting the chart into a different key. A chart's numbers maintain their same relationship with a song's chord changes regardless of the key."

> "Musicians used the number system to chart out an arrangement while hearing a demo of the tune for the first time."

## Primary claims

- Charts numéricos criados na primeira audição de demo.
- Relação entre números e mudanças permanece invariante ao tom.
- Entrada mínima: identificar "1" e contar batidas por compasso.
