## Verbatim snippets / quotes

> "The characteristic Brazilian harmonic device of using a minor 6 chord (C-6) as a substitute for a dominant chord (F7) is evident here."

> "One of Jobim's signature techniques is the use of diminished chords as passing harmonies. The diminished 7 chord can resolve in multiple directions, adding harmonic flexibility."

> "The Imaj7 to I-7 movement (Dmaj7 to D-7) is a classic Jobim technique, shifting from major to minor within the same tonal center."

## Primary claims

- Acorde m6 funciona como dominante substituto (ex.: Cm6 → F).
- Diminutos como passagem cromática (A°7 → Bbmaj7).
- Alternância maj7/min7 na mesma tônica é recurso Jobim.
- Internalizar esses "atalhos" brasileiros acelera inferência em MPB.
